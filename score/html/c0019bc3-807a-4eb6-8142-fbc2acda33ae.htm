      Negotiation Regulations

      You may negotiate with or for any card in the deck. Negotiations can only be initiated by or with the player who has drawn from the Grass Stack and hold seven cards.

      The basic negotiations open are:

      1. Direct hand to hand, one card for one card exchanges. Remember, each player may only hold six cards in his hand for play to continue.

      2. You may negotiate for and with tabled Peddle money. In this case, payment is made only from one Stash Pile to another Stash Pile. (One or more Peddle cards may be exchanged.)

      3. You may negotiate a combination deal of (1) and (2) but when completed, each player must still have six cards in his hand.

      4. You may not negotiate for any cards in the Wasted Pile.
