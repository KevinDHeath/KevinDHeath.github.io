      G

      Grass Stack

      All Draw cards are obtained from here.

      H

      Hand

      A game can consist of multiple hands until a winner is declared. It requires all the cards to be thoroughly shuffled and placed face down. When all the cards in the pile are used, or someone plays a Market Close, the hand is over.

      Hassle Pile

      Each player starts one with a Market Open card. Opponents put "heat" on your Hassle Pile. You "un-heat" yourself with "heat of" cards so you can build your Stash with Peddle cards.

      R

      Round

      A hand consists of multiple rounds where each player takes a card from the Grass Stack and makes a play.

      S

      Stash Pile

      Each player's Peddle cards are placed here in full view of all players. This ia also known as the player's marketplace.

      W

      Wasted Pile

      All Discards go here. Only Nirvana and Paranoia cards come into play when placed here.
