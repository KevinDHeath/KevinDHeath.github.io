      How to Score Each Hand

      1. Each player totals the Peddle Money in their Stash Pile.

      2. If the Banker card is held then that player skims 20% off the unprotected tabled Peddle cards in each opponents' Stash Pile. Opponents subtract the skim amount from their scores.

      3. Each player subtracts amount of the highest single Peddle card held in their hand.

      4. Each player subtracts total of fines of the Paranoia cards held in their hand.

      5. Total net score for the hand.

      6. $25,000 bonus goes to the player with the highest net score for that hand.

      Gone broke? If your end-of-round total is less than zero, you can round it up to 0. If you're in profit in a first round, but negative in the next, your total goes down accordingly. At the start of each round, broke accounts are generously rounded up to 0.
